About metaFrazo

Our Mission

Breaking down language barriers by making premium content localization accessible to creators and businesses worldwide through advanced AI technology.

Our Innovation

Revolutionary lip-sync technology that preserves original voices while seamlessly translating speech and on-screen text for authentic global experiences.

Our Promise

Delivering faster, more cost-effective content localization without compromising quality, enabling creators to reach global audiences effortlessly.

Our Services

AI Voice Lip-Sync

Advanced lip-sync technology that maintains natural mouth movements while translating speech, preserving the authenticity of original performances.

Voice Preservation Translation

Translate content while keeping the original speaker's voice characteristics, tone, and emotional delivery intact across multiple languages.

On-Screen Text Translation

Accurate translation and replacement of text overlays, subtitles, and graphics while maintaining visual consistency and timing.

Rapid Content Localization

Complete end-to-end localization pipeline that delivers professional-quality translated content in a fraction of traditional timeframes.
